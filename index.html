Inward Safari: How do you feel?

Joel Casey / YouTube
Getting to know yourself is so vital to how you experience life. If you're in alignment with your true nature, you're tendency will be to feel good. If you're out of alignment, through misperceptions, your tendency will be to feel bad.Check in with yourself throughout the day and ask: "how do I feel right now?"

Inward Safari Self Responsibility

YouTube
How we interpret and perceive an event determines the way we feel. Notice throughout your day how you interpret your experiences. Never beat yourself up for misperceiving an event and thus feeling like you have no choice but to fall victim and feel helpless, hopeless and powerless.
